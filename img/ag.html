What to Expect

Our Academic Guides are perfect for helping you navigate and overcome obstacles in the course or courses that are historically challenging for most students. Because the guides are fellow students, they were once in the exact spot you may find yourself in now! If there's anyone who truly understands, it's your peers! The goal of each session is to help you wherever you feel you could improve. You explain what you want to work on, and we make it our job to strengthen the scaffolding that makes success possible!

Partnering for your success

Our Academic Guides are perfect for helping you navigate and overcome obstacles in the course or courses that are historically challenging for most students. Because the guides are fellow students, they were once in the exact spot you may find yourself in now! If there's anyone who truly understands, it's your peers! Our Academic Guides have taken the classes they support and have practiced the material. They want to guide other students to practice the material and feel more confident, too. Academic Guides can share study tips, answer questions, and suggest practice problems, all to strengthen your own study habits and understanding!​

LRC graded assignment policy

Tutors are prohibited from assisting students with any assignments that will be submitted for a grade. The tutor can clarify concepts related to an assignment and/or do similar practice problems. Please view the CU Denver Academic Integrity Policy for more information.

Guided Study Groups

Important — Please read the LRC's Expectations prior to your appointment

90 minute sessions | Course Specific
Study together, review concepts, practice problems, work together and learn from each other, support each other, be more prepared for class, strengthen your understanding, form a community!

*Sessions may be online or in person, based on availability. Students can indicate preference, but we do not guarantee a specific format.

Sign up is first come, first served. There are limited spots, so sign up early!*

Question Sessions

Important — Please read the LRC's Expectations prior to your appointment

Check in at the Learning Hub front desk, or register when signing onto Zoom for an Online Appointment. Show up at any time during the open session.
You can drop in any time with a quick question or a specific concept you want clarified. The academic guide will rotate between you and other students to clarify concepts. The guide can also direct you to practice problems or give study tips. After your question is answered, you can leave. Or, you can stay and continue studying and ask more questions as they come up.

*Sessions may be online or in person, based on availability.

×

Boost Pilot Program

Important — Please read the LRC's Expectations prior to your appointment

BOOST sessions are held once a month.

The Academic Guide will lead a review of the difficult concepts and include a chance for students to practice with material. Students will meet classmates and work together in interactive components. This includes large group learning and small group work to help better prepare you for future lectures and exams. Don't fall behind on difficult concepts, sign up today!

*Sessions may be online or in person, based on availability.
