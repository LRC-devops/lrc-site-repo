LRC graded assignment policy

Tutors are prohibited from assisting students with any assignments that will be submitted for a grade. The tutor can clarify concepts related to an assignment and/or do similar practice problems. Please view the CU Denver Academic Integrity Policy for more information.

For Faculty and staff

The Learning Resources Center can be a valuable resource to faculty and staff at CU Denver. We offer a series of study skills workshops which can be presented to your class to assist in developing their learning potential. Visit our Faculty and Staff Resources page to learn more about what we can do for you!
