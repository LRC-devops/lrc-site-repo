Gen-Chem O-Chem

Refresh Your Memory

Has it been a while since you took chemistry? Are your math skills a little rusty? Don't worry, most people struggle to remember everything about a subject when they've been out of practice. But there's no need to struggle. Intense Preparation (I-Prep) can get you up to speed and ready to jump back in before the semester starts.
Registration is required. Don't wait; spaces are limited!


What is iPrep?

I-Prep focuses on core knowledge areas and key concepts to maximize your success. They take place weeks before the semester starts.



At I-Prep Sessions You Will

  • Work with peers to learn key concepts, complete practice problems similar to those in class
  • Solidify your understanding of foundational knowledge so you feel ready to take the course
  • Build transferable study strategies
  • Receive additional course-related materials to reference throughout the semester
  • Meet classmates, opportunity to form study groups

You Should Register for I-Prep if

  • You know you will be taking CHEM 1000 or CHEM 2031, CHEM 3411, PBHL 3001, a math class, or any other subject we offer I-Prep sessions for.
  • You will be taking the General Chemistry Assessment Test (GCAT)
  • You want to brush up your math skills
  • You want to learn or practice how to use a scientific calculator
